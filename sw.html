SMALL DOG BREEDS

Description

Corgi

The Pembroke Welsh Corgi is a powerful, athletic, and vivacious little herder who is loving and companionable without being needy, making him one of the most delightful small housedogs. They belong to the most well-liked herding breeds in the world.

Pug

The small but sturdy Pug is now adored by his millions of fans throughout the world. He was once the mischievous companion of Chinese emperors and later served as the mascot of Holland's royal House of Orange. Pugs are creatures of love and desire.

Beagle

In addition to being a fantastic hunter and devoted friend, the Beagle is also cheerful, humorous, and, thanks to its begging expression, adorable. Since they were developed to hunt in groups, they are sociable and generally laid-back.
